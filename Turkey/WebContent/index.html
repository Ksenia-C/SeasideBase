Seaside Base

This website was made for the organization of information. Are you an organizer of some repeating event and would like to see statistics of participations? Or are you school teacher and would like to find out in what school activities your students are involved? Then this site can help you by keep statistics.

It can collect data from csv tables, creating one common base for every person and every event and participation.

So, how to work with the site? Tables can be loaded to the site in the section "add table". The section "for people" is for tables with information about persons. There can be name, surname, degree or number of group and some other common data. It supposes that every person can be identified with a low probability of repetitions by Name, Surname, Date of Birth, City and maybe Patronymic.

The section "with event" is for tables with some results or preparations for the events. Form of registration or scores for tasks. You also may state in what columns of table what main information about human is, so to try to find participators from base.

To search among people you need to open the section "make request/about people". The same for events is in the "make request/ about events". To see all the added items, don't fill any fields. One-click on the string leads to the page of item (person or event). On that page, all the information about it and data can be changed.
